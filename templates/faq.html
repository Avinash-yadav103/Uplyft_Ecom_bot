Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about ShopAssist

General Questions

What is ShopAssist?
ShopAssist is an e-commerce platform with an integrated AI assistant that helps you find the perfect products based on your preferences and needs. Our intelligent chatbot can recommend products, answer questions, and guide you through the shopping experience.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team by clicking the "Help & FAQ" button in our chatbot, sending an email to support@shopassist.com, or calling us at 1-800-SHOP-NOW during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM ET).
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties without your consent. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Products & Shopping

How do I find a specific product?
You can use the search bar at the top of our website, browse product categories, or ask our ShopAssist chatbot to help you find specific items. Simply describe what you're looking for, and our AI will suggest relevant products.
Are the product images accurate?
We strive to provide accurate product images that represent the actual items. However, colors may appear slightly different depending on your display settings. For the most accurate representation, we recommend reading the detailed product descriptions and customer reviews.
Do you offer size guides for clothing?
Yes, each clothing item has a size guide accessible from the product page. You can also ask our ShopAssist chatbot for sizing recommendations based on your measurements and preferences.

Orders & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For certain promotions, we also accept store credit and gift cards.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status by logging into your account or asking our ShopAssist chatbot about your recent orders.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement. After that, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be canceled, but you can return it following our return policy.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is 1-2 business days. International shipping varies by destination but generally takes 7-14 business days. Actual delivery times may vary based on your location.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see the specific shipping options available to your country during checkout.
Is free shipping available?
Yes, we offer free standard shipping on orders over $50 within the continental United States. Premium members receive free shipping on all orders regardless of order value.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items in new, unworn condition with original tags and packaging. Some items like underwear, swimwear, and final sale items cannot be returned for hygiene or other reasons.
How do I return an item?
To initiate a return, log into your account and select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return. Follow the prompts to generate a return label. You can also ask our ShopAssist chatbot to help you with the return process.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive your return, it typically takes 3-5 business days to inspect and process. After processing, refunds are issued to your original payment method and may take an additional 2-10 business days to appear, depending on your financial institution.

ShopAssist Chatbot Help

What can the ShopAssist chatbot help me with?
Our ShopAssist chatbot can help you find products, answer questions about items, provide personalized recommendations, check order status, assist with returns, and much more. Think of it as your personal shopping assistant!
Is the chatbot available 24/7?
Yes, the ShopAssist chatbot is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Human customer support representatives are available during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM ET).
What if the chatbot can't answer my question?
If the chatbot is unable to resolve your issue, it will offer to connect you with a human customer service representative during business hours, or you can email our support team at support@shopassist.com.
